Good practices

What makes a campaign thrive

A few simple things help your story reach — and move — more people.

Tell a true story

Write honestly and from the heart. Explain who it's for, what's needed, and exactly how support will help.

Use good photos

Real, clear images build trust instantly. A warm, well-lit photo helps people connect with your cause.

Set a realistic goal

An achievable target feels within reach and encourages people to help you cross the line.

Share widely

Send your page to friends and family first. Early support builds momentum that draws in more.

Update supporters

Post honest progress and thank-yous. Keeping people in the loop keeps the support — and trust — growing.
