From finding a story to seeing your impact — giving on Spread Hope is simple, secure and transparent at every step.

Step by step

What happens at each step

Find a story

Search by category, urgency or place. Every campaign is reviewed before it appears, so you can give with confidence.

Read the story

Get to know the person and the need — photos, the goal, the progress so far, and who is organizing it.

Choose your amount

Pick what feels right and see exactly how your gift moves the campaign forward. No hidden pressure, ever.

Confirm securely

Review and confirm. Your contribution is processed securely and directed toward the campaign's stated goal.

See your impact

Get a confirmation, follow the progress bar climb, and share the story so it reaches even more people who can help.
