Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to run Spread Hope and keep it trustworthy. This includes information you provide directly and a limited amount of information collected automatically.

Information you give us

  • Account details such as your name and email address.
  • Campaign content you choose to publish, including your story and photos.
  • Messages you send to our support team.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic device and browser information.
  • Usage data that helps us improve the platform.

How we use your data

We use your information to operate the platform, review campaigns, communicate with you, and keep Spread Hope safe. We do not sell your personal data.

  • To publish and manage your campaigns.
  • To review fundraisers and prevent misuse.
  • To send you essential updates about your account or campaign.

How we protect your data

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information. Access to personal data is limited to those who need it to operate or support the platform.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies to keep the site working and to understand how it's used. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as well without them.

Your choices and rights

You can access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under local privacy law. To make a request, contact us through the details below.

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate details.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the platform, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, we remove or anonymize it.

Contact us about privacy

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please reach us through the contact page. We're happy to help.
