Privacy Policy

A short, clear summary of what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Spread Hope helps people give and raise funds simply and safely. This policy explains, in plain language, what data we handle and how. We collect only what we need, we’re clear about why, and we don’t sell your personal data. It works together with our Terms of Service.

What we collect

We collect information you give us and a small amount collected automatically as you use the Platform:

  • your name and email address;
  • account details and preferences;
  • campaign information, images, stories, and updates you publish;
  • messages, comments, and words of support you post;
  • donation-related details, such as the amount, campaign, and date;
  • cookies and basic technical data, like your device, browser, and approximate location.

How we use your data

We use your information to run Spread Hope and keep it trustworthy — to display and manage campaigns, process donations, send you essential updates, provide support, prevent fraud, improve the experience, and meet legal obligations.

Payments

Donations are handled by trusted third-party payment providers under their own terms and security standards. Your card and banking details are collected and processed by them — Spread Hope does not store full payment details.

Cookies and analytics

We use a small number of cookies and analytics tools to keep the site working, remember your preferences, and understand how it’s used so we can improve it. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as well without them.

Sharing with necessary services

We share data only where needed to operate the Platform — with payment providers, hosting, analytics, and security partners that are bound to protect it. We may also share information with authorities when required by law or to prevent fraud or harm. We never sell your personal data.

Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, and we limit access to those who need it. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your data and to respond quickly if an issue arises.

Your rights

You can access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal data, and adjust your communication preferences. To make a request, just contact us — we may need to verify your identity first, and some data may be kept where the law requires.

Public or anonymous donations

Campaigns are public. When you donate, you can choose to appear publicly — showing your name, amount, and any message — or to give anonymously, which hides your name from the public display. Either way, we still process the transaction securely behind the scenes. Anything you post publicly can be seen by others, so share only what you’re comfortable making public.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or your data? Reach us anytime through the contact page — we’re happy to help.

Thank you for trusting Spread Hope with your story and your support. 🌱
